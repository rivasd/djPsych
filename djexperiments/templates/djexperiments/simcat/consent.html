[-] Objectives and remuneration

The objective of the present study is to investigate the effects of corrective feedback on trial-and-error category learning

Participants will be remunerated proportionally to their performace up to a maximum of 20$. Payments through Paypal or in person at our lab (email us to schedule)

[-] Risks and Benefits

No risk, to your health or otherwise, has been linked to the participation in this study. It is carried out entirely in your browser and can be stopped at any time simply by closing the current tab.

[-] Confidentiality and Anonymity

It is understood that all data collected (key presses, reaction times) is confidential. Only the members of the research team and interns will have access to the data. To protect your identity, you will be identified by a participant number known only to us.
The only personal information collected is your email address, that will also be kept confidential and never sold, transmitted, or used for spam.
All data will be kept only for the duration of the research project and will be subsequently destroyed.
No data pertaining to your IP adress or location is collected, and a cookie is given to your browser to enable you to login automatically and save your language preferences. All JavaScript source code is easily viewable in the browser.

[-] Voluntary Consent and Righ to Retire

Your participation in this experiment is completely voluntary. This means that you accept to take part in it under no constraint or external pressure.
You are free to retire from the experiment at any time.
Your informed consent also implies that you accept that the research team may use all collected data in service of the present project, including activities such as articles, student theses or paper, conferences, and scientific communications, this at the condition that no information that could identify you will be divulgated without your explicit consent.

[-] Responsibility clause
